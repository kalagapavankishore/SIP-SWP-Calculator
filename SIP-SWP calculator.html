Kishore's Mutual Fund Retirement Planner

How to Use:

Initial Lumpsum Investment: Enter the amount you are investing as a one-time deposit.

Monthly SIP Amount: Amount you plan to invest every month.

Expected Annual Return (%): The estimated yearly return percentage on your investment.

Return Decline per Year: Enter 4 or 5, as returns generally decrease over time.

SIP Increment per Year (%): Percentage increase in SIP amount yearly.

Number of Years for SIP: Duration for which you plan to invest in SIP.

Monthly SWP Amount: Amount you plan to withdraw monthly during retirement.

Number of Years for SWP: How long you want to withdraw money.

SWP Increment per Year (%): Yearly increase in withdrawal amount.
